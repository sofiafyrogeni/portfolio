1.3: Calculate the total daily rate of employees who have the highest performance rating and work overtime.
1.4: Find the average distance from home for employees who are in a managerial role and have been with their current manager for more than 5 years.

1.6: Find the number of employees who have been in their current role for more than 5 years but haven't been promoted in the last 2 years.
1.7: Calculate the average monthly income of employees who are married, have been at the company for more than 10 years, and are in Research & Development.
